breakfast potatoes 1

pan-roasted potatoes made with turmeric and garlic

Posted by Tara on January 1, 2017

I don't know anybody that doesn't like potatoes, at least in some form or other. And that's the beauty of potatoes: its versatility. So in honor of that, we present the breakfast potato series! Enjoy!

Notes

serves 3-4 preptime: 10 min cooktime: 15 min totaltime: 25 min

Ingredients

  • 3 russet potatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 2 tbsp pre-roasted red pepper, chopped
  • pinch of salt
  • coriander, optional garnish
  • 3 unopened chili peppers, optional
  • 1/2 tsp turmuric, optional
  • pinch of black pepper
Ingredients

Steps

Warm the pan at medium-high heat. Once warm, add 2 tbsp of olive oil to coat the pan evenly. Then, add a touch of butter.

Put in the cumin, shake pan until it stops sizzling, then put in the sliced garlic. Shake for 30 seconds, until the garlic starts to lightly brown. Put in the chopped onions, along with a pinch of salt.

After a minute of stirring, add the potatoes and stir until they are coating with the oil/onion mixture. Sprinkle two more pinches of salt on top with 1/2 tsp of turmuric and stir. Then, add the three unopened chili peppers for gentle heat.

Cover and let sit on medium-high heat for 10 minutes. Stir occasionally.

Add salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with coriander. Enjoy!
